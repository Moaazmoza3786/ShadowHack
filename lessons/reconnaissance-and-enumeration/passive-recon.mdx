
# Passive Reconnaissance: OSINT & Google Dorking

## The Art of Electronic Warfare
Before you ever touch a target's server with a packet, you can often compromise them using only public information. This is **Open Source Intelligence (OSINT)**.

In this phase, we do **not** touch the target's infrastructure. We query third-party sources (Google, Shodan, LinkedIn, DNS Registrars).

## 1. Google Dorking (Google Hacking)
Google indexes everything, including sensitive files that admins accidentally exposed. "Dorking" is the use of advanced search operators.

### Critical Operators
- `site:` - Limit results to a specific domain.
- `filetype:` - Look for specific extensions (pdf, xlsx, docx, config).
- `intitle:` - Search for text in the page title.
- `inurl:` - Search for text in the URL structure.
