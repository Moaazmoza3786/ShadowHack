## Mission Briefing

We don't do "classrooms" here. We do **Operations**. 
BreachLabs is an elite cyberpunk training ground designed to forge raw talent into tier-one security operators. 

Your journey will take you from the basics of the Linux terminal to the depths of kernel exploitation. You will learn to think like an attacker to better defend the infrastructure of tomorrow.

> **Warning:** Everything you learn here is a weapon. Use it only on systems you own or have explicit permission to test. Unauthorized access is illegal and violates the BreachLabs Code of Conduct.
